About the Workshop

The workhop Topics in Random Media will be held in Santiago, Chile on Thursday and Friday before the SPA of Buenos Aires. It will feature minicourses on RWRE by A. Fribergh and Ch. Sabot, and a minicourse on Regularity Structures by L. Zambotti. In addition, there will be a small number of talks.

Please, refer to the schedule on the right for titles and abstracts.

Registration and Funding

Registration is free but mandatory. Please, contact the organizers to let us know you intend to participate. We also have funding for several graduate students from Southamerica. Please, submit any request before June 10th.

Practical Information

The talk will be held at the department of mathematics of PUC-Chile. Have a look at the following link for information on housing and how to get here.
